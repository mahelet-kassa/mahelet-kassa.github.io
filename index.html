My journey

The intersections of business and technology have always fascinated me. Bloomberg Terminal was my first introduction to the increasingly important role that technology plays in businesses’ operations. Having completed a bachelor’s in business administration, concentrating in technology operations management, I became even more interested in software engineering and the immense impact that technology has on the world. I decided to take a risk and the potential failure that goes with embarking on any new course of action and enrolled in General Assembly’s Full Stack Web Development Immersive, successfully completing the course in December 2019.

As a full stack software engineer skilled in several programming languages and frameworks, I actively seek out new technologies and stay up-to-date on the industry trends and advancements. My hope is to build applications that will leverage companies’ productivity, efficiency, and social impact, furthering my passion and vision of applying technology to business for social good.
